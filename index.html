Cha Cafè & Bistro:

Paul's Website Proposal

This will be a website referring to a local shop in Saipan that serves drinks, pasteries, and breakfast items. The reason I chose this project is because most of the Cafè's here in Saipan use social media to share their menu and updates. A real website will help Cha Cafè and Bistro look more professional and make it easier for locals and tourists to see the menu, hours, and location. The goal is to help the cafè get more customers, show their promotions, and build a stronger online presense that matches the vibes of Saipan.

Please view the provided html links to see their certain hours, locations, menu items, and much more.
